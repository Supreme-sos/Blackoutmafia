Welcome (Read First)

Blackout Mafia is a creative community centered on loyalty, events, and member projects. This group is NOT about violence, intimidation, or illegal activities. We do not tolerate harm — we build bonds, hold meetups, and create content. Anyone joining agrees to follow our code of conduct and respect local laws.

How to Join — Simple Steps

  1. Follow both our official Instagram handles: @blackoutmafia_ and @xv.shadow_triad.
  2. DM either handle with a short intro: your name, why you want to join, and what you can contribute (art, events, moderation, design).
  3. Get Vouched — new members need 1-2 existing members to vouch, or pass a short interview with a leader.
  4. Sign the Oath — a non-legal affirmation to follow the code and act responsibly.
  5. Attend a welcome meetup or group call (virtual) to be activated.

Membership Requirements

  • Be respectful and cooperative.
  • Do not promote violence, hate speech, or illegal activity.
  • Contribute at least once per month (post, event, or help).
  • Keep internal information private.

Code of Conduct (Short)

We expect members to act like adults: no doxxing, do not threaten others, follow event safety, and use group channels responsibly. Violations lead to warnings and removal.

Events & Activities

We run creative meetups, charity drives, design collabs, and member spotlights. If you have event ideas — pitch them in the group.

“We are shadows who build, not shadows who harm.”
